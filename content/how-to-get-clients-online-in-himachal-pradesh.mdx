## Step 2 – Set Up Your Google Business Profile

Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is free and powerful for local businesses in Paonta Sahib and Himachal Pradesh.

When someone searches "interior designer near Paonta Sahib," the map results that appear are all Google Business Profiles.

To set it up:
1. Go to business.google.com
2. Add your business name, category, and address
3. Upload photos of your work or premises
4. Get at least 5–10 genuine customer reviews

This alone can start bringing inquiries within weeks.
