## What's Not Included in Website Costs

Always ask your developer what's included. Common add-ons that cost extra:

- **Domain name** – ₹800–₹1,500/year (e.g., .in or .com)
- **Hosting** – ₹3,000–₹15,000/year depending on traffic
- **SSL certificate** – Often included with hosting
- **Content writing** – Copywriting for all pages
- **Logo design** – If you don't have one
- **Monthly maintenance** – Ongoing updates and support

When working with me, **Mohammad Altaf**, I clearly lay out all costs upfront. No surprises.
