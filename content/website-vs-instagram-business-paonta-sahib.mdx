## Where Instagram Falls Short

But here's what Instagram cannot do — and this is where most business owners in Paonta Sahib get stuck:

### You Don't Own Your Audience
Your followers belong to Instagram. If the platform changes its algorithm (which it does, frequently), your organic reach drops overnight — and you have no control.

### You Can't Rank on Google
Instagram profiles do not rank well on Google for local service searches. When someone searches "carpenter in Paonta Sahib," they search Google — not Instagram.

### No Credibility for Certain Clients
Larger clients, corporate buyers, and professionals expect a business to have a website. An Instagram-only business raises trust concerns.

### Limited Information
Instagram is not built for long-form service descriptions, pricing, detailed portfolios, or contact forms. A website handles all of this.

### Instagram Can Ban Your Account
Accounts get hacked, falsely flagged, or banned. Overnight, you can lose all your followers and content. It has happened to real businesses in Paonta Sahib and across India.

Think of Instagram as your marketing channel. Think of your website as your business home.
