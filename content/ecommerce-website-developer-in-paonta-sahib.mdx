## What Makes a Good Ecommerce Website

A lot of e-commerce websites look decent but fail in practice. Here's what actually matters:

### Fast Loading Speed
Slow websites lose customers. If your store takes more than 3 seconds to load, a large percentage of visitors will leave. I optimize every store I build for performance.

### Secure Payments
Customers need to trust that their payment information is safe. I integrate trusted payment gateways like **Razorpay** and **Stripe** — both reliable and compliant with Indian regulations.

### Mobile-First Design
Most online shoppers in Paonta Sahib and Himachal Pradesh browse on mobile devices. Every store I build is fully responsive and easy to use on smartphones.

### Simple Product Management
You should be able to add, edit, and remove products without calling a developer. I build admin panels that are simple enough for anyone to use.

### SEO for Product Discovery
Your products need to show up on Google. I structure product pages, categories, and metadata so search engines can find and rank your store.
