## Reason 1: Your Website Was Never Submitted to Google

Google doesn't automatically find new websites. You need to submit your sitemap to **Google Search Console** so it knows your site exists.

**Fix:** Create a Google Search Console account, verify your website, and submit your sitemap.xml file. Without this, your site is invisible.

---

## Reason 2: No Local SEO Optimization

If you're targeting customers in Paonta Sahib, your website needs to signal that clearly to Google.

Signs of missing local SEO:
- No mention of "Paonta Sahib" in your page titles or content
- No Google Business Profile set up
- No local schema markup

**Fix:** Add your city name on key pages, set up Google Business Profile, and add LocalBusiness schema markup to your homepage.

---

## Reason 3: Your Website is Too Slow

Page speed is a confirmed Google ranking factor. A slow website hurts rankings — and drives visitors away.

Common causes of slow websites in Paonta Sahib:
- Uncompressed images
- Cheap shared hosting
- Too many plugins (common with WordPress)
- No caching

**Fix:** Compress images, use fast hosting (Vercel, Hostinger Pro), and optimize your code. A site that loads in under 2 seconds will outperform competitors.

## Reason 4: Your Content is Too Thin or Generic

Google doesn't rank pages that don't say anything useful. A home page with 50 words and generic text like "Welcome to our website" will not rank for anything.

**Fix:** Write real, helpful content. If you're a plumber in Paonta Sahib, write about what you offer, which areas you serve, how much it costs, and what customers ask most. Be specific.

## Reason 5: You're Targeting Keywords That Are Too Competitive

Trying to rank for "best developer in India" as a new business is like opening a shop and expecting to beat Amazon. You need to start with realistic, local keywords.

**Fix:** Target long-tail local keywords: "electrician near Paonta Sahib", "wedding photographer Paonta Sahib Himachal Pradesh". These are easier to rank and bring higher-intent customers.

## Reason 6: Your Website Has No Backlinks

Backlinks — other websites linking to yours — are one of the strongest ranking signals on Google. A brand new website with zero backlinks will struggle to rank even with perfect on-page SEO.

**Fix:** Get listed in local directories (Just Dial, IndiaMART, Sulekha). Ask business partners or local associations to link to your site. Write guest posts. Start building authority over time.

---

## Reason 7: Your Website is Not Mobile-Friendly

Google uses mobile-first indexing — meaning it primarily looks at the mobile version of your website. If your site breaks on a phone, it will rank poorly.

**Fix:** Test your site at Google's Mobile-Friendly Test tool. Make sure all text is readable without zooming, buttons are tappable, and images are properly sized on small screens.

---

## Reason 8: You Stopped After Launch

SEO is not a one-time task. Most businesses in Paonta Sahib build a website and never touch it again. Google favors websites that are regularly updated with fresh, relevant content.

**Fix:** Start a blog. Write one article per week about topics your customers search for in Paonta Sahib. Consistency compounds into rankings over time.
