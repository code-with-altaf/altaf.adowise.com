## Step 1 – Validate Your Idea Before Investing

The biggest mistake early entrepreneurs in Paonta Sahib make is spending money on branding, websites, and setup before confirming anyone wants what they're selling.

**How to validate quickly:**
- Talk to 10–20 potential customers in Paonta Sahib before spending anything
- Ask if they'd pay for your solution

- See if they actually use it and stick around

Only after validation should you build the full product or service infrastructure.

## Step 2 – Register Your Business

For businesses in Himachal Pradesh, here are the common registration types:

- **Sole Proprietorship** – Simplest option for individual service businesses. Low cost, minimal paperwork.
- **Private Limited Company (Pvt Ltd)** – Best for startups that plan to scale, raise funding, or work with large clients.
- **LLP (Limited Liability Partnership)** – Good middle ground between sole proprietorship and Pvt Ltd.

You'll also need:
- A **GST registration** if annual revenue exceeds ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs for states like Himachal Pradesh in some categories)
- A **Udyam Registration** (MSME) for government benefits
- A **current account** in your business name for professional invoicing
