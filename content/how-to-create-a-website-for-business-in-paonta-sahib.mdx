## Step 1 – Define Your Website's Purpose

Before anything else, get clear on why you need the website. Most businesses in Paonta Sahib need one of these:

- **Lead generation** – Get people to call or message you
- **Credibility** – Show that your business is legitimate
- **E-commerce** – Sell products or services online
- **Information** – Help customers find your location, hours, and services

Your purpose shapes every decision after this.

---

## Step 2 – Decide What Pages You Need

A typical business website for a Paonta Sahib company should have:

- **Home** – Who you are, what you do, why choose you
- **Services** – Detailed list of what you offer
- **About** – Your story, team, or background
- **Contact** – Phone, email, location, and a contact form
- **Blog** (optional but strongly recommended) – For SEO and attracting organic traffic

Keep it simple at first. You can always add more later.

---

## Step 3 – Choose a Domain Name

Your domain is your website address. For example: `yourbusiness.com` or `yourbusiness.in`.

Tips for choosing a domain:
- Keep it short and easy to remember
- Use your business name if possible
- Prefer `.com` or `.in` for Indian businesses
- Avoid hyphens and numbers if possible

Domain registration typically costs ₹800–₹1,500 per year.

---

## Step 4 – Get Reliable Hosting

Your website needs to live on a server. Hosting is what makes it accessible online.

For businesses in Paonta Sahib, I generally recommend:
- **Vercel or Netlify** for modern Next.js websites (fast and free for smaller sites)
- **Hostinger or SiteGround** for WordPress-based websites

Good hosting makes your website load faster — which directly affects SEO rankings.

---

## Step 5 – Design the Website

Design is not just about looking good. It's about making it easy for visitors to take action.

Key design principles for Paonta Sahib businesses:
- Mobile-first design (most local searches happen on phones)
- Clear navigation — visitors should find what they need in 2 clicks
- Visible contact information on every page
- A clear call-to-action (Call Now, Get a Quote, Book an Appointment)

---

## Step 6 – Write the Content

Content is what Google reads to decide whether your website should rank. It's also what visitors read to decide whether to trust you.

Tips for writing website content:
- Write for your customer, not for search engines
- Use natural language, the way you'd talk to a customer in Paonta Sahib
- Include keywords like your service + location (e.g., "plumber in Paonta Sahib")
- Avoid copy-pasting from other websites

## Step 7 – Set Up Basic SEO

Before launching, make sure these basics are in place:

- Title tag and meta description on each page
- Google Analytics to track visitors
- Google Search Console to submit your sitemap
- Google Business Profile to appear in local map results

This is the minimum SEO setup for any business in Paonta Sahib, Himachal Pradesh.
