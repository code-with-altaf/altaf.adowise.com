AI image generation has exploded in 2026. What started as blurry faces and distorted hands has evolved into photorealistic masterpieces that rival professional photography.

## Top AI Image Generators

### 1. Midjourney v7
- **Quality**: Industry-leading photorealism and artistic style
- **Pricing**: $10/month (Basic), $30/month (Standard)
- **Best For**: Marketing visuals, concept art, social media content

### 2. DALL-E 3 (via ChatGPT)
- **Quality**: Excellent text rendering, strong prompt adherence
- **Pricing**: Included with ChatGPT Plus ($20/month)
- **Best For**: Quick mockups, blog images, presentations

### 3. Leonardo AI
- **Quality**: Great for game assets and consistent character design
- **Pricing**: Free tier available, Pro at $12/month
- **Best For**: Game developers, consistent brand imagery

### 4. Stable Diffusion (Open Source)
- **Quality**: Highly customizable with fine-tuning
- **Pricing**: Free (requires local GPU or cloud compute)
- **Best For**: Developers, researchers, privacy-conscious users

## Free vs Paid: Real Differences

Free tools work for casual use, but paid tools offer higher resolution, faster generation, commercial licensing, and better prompt understanding. For any professional or commercial use, investing in a paid tier is essential.

## Conclusion

The AI image generation space is maturing rapidly. The key is choosing the right tool for your specific workflow rather than chasing the "best" one overall.
